How Can I Help You?.

If you need a website, app, logo, graphics or brand design, send me an email with your brief. I will get back to you as soon as possible!

UI Design.

I have worked on several user interface design projects for large companies including Echelon Networks and Hampshire County Council.

UX Design.

Designing a satisfying digital experience is a hugely important part of any project. I have had years of experience creating a practical yet meaningful experience. Feel free to ask if you would like me to work on the UX base of your project or intergrate this with my ability to create UI design

Photography.

I have been taking photographs since I was young. I have recently done some Fashion photography at Bath Fashion Show and have completed various Photography experiments intergrating the use of Photoshop and Lighbox. Examples of my photography work can be found in the section above.

Branding.

I have had huge experience branding companies. I have run my own record label ATOMA RECORDS for close to three years now with a business partner. I have had to brand the company and follow a theme throughout the years which has slowly expanded into something I could never have imagined. I have also helped run and brand the large music company MUSIC4MONTAGE which no has over 200,000 supporters.

Web Design.

As well as this website, I have designed and coded several others for companies such as Human Values Foundation and Hampshire county council projects. "Effective web design is clean and gets to the point without misleading the user". That is what I believe. I have also designed and managed my own companies site for over three years, allowing my knowledge of design to expand the site gradually.

Graphics.

With years of graphic and photoshop experience I am able to mock up and design to the clients desire, quickly and effectively.

About Me.

I am a modern and enthusiastic designer that encorporates the endless rules of design and layout with a clean and simple mindset. I have designed from a young age and still get a huge buzz when a project finally comes together. I currently study Digital Media Design & Development at the University of Winchester and have already gained an extensive skill set which has been applied to various client projects. I have worked with Hampshire county council, Echelon Networks, Universities and large scale music companies.

My Design Approach.

I like to work closely with clients allowing them to gain insight into the stages in which I've taken to complete a project. Simple, clean and effective are the three words I like to use when thinking web design.

Background.

Originally from Devon, I now live in Winchester after moving here at 18 to study at the University. I founded an electronic record label when I was 17 which I still run with my good friend from College in Exeter. I have designed album covers and digital material for the label since it was established giving me an impecable insight into the world of design in music. I was always good at illustration and thinking. During my University Years I have enhanced my skillset dramatically and am now working with some of the newest technologies and software available.
