How Can I Help You?.

If you need a website, app, logo, graphics or brand design, send me an email with your brief. I will get back to you as soon as possible!

UI Design.

Good User Interface (UI) is important for any website or app ensuring your product stands out from your competitors. It needs to be simple to use. I have been involved in several major projects including creating UI layouts for Hampshire County Councils Energy Saving Project as well as Echelon Networks school review App.

UX Design.

A perfect UX or "User Experience" design is what makes your customer return. I have had years of designing User Experience.

Photography.

I have recently been involved with Fashion photography at Bath fashion show and have completed various photography experiments intergrating the use of Photoshop and Lighbox. I have also had experience in the latest photographic technology, using the Lytro Illum and changing focal points to create multiple results, including Living Pictures, 3D lenticular prints and stereoscopic side-by-side 3D animations.

Branding.

I have years of experience branding companies. I have run my own record label ATOMA RECORDS for three years now. This involves marketing, designing and dealing with finance. I also assist with running and branding the large music company MUSIC4MONTAGE which now has over 250,000 supporters.

Web Design.

Web design is the digital face of your company. I always focus on the simplicity and ease of a sight before anything else. I code in Sublime Text 2 and currently host my sites on Github. Shoot me an email if you'd like to enquire about a site.

Graphics.

I am able to quickly and effectively mock up and design to a clients brief. I am compitent in the use of Illustrator and After Effects and can use these to take your graphics further into 2D animation and printed.

My Client Approach.

I like to work closely with the client, keeping you updated on everything that's happening during the build of your project. Planning and research will always take place before the production to make sure all the final products I create are seamless.
