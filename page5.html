Skateboard Designs.

Following a splash of graphic creativity I decided to design a series of skateboard decks. This project was just for my own amusement during the summer of 2015. I love the idea of customization within any form of sports. Clothing, equipment etc.

I looked into some of the the major skateboard brands/sponsors Monster, Skatehouse and DC. I decided to base my designs on these companies and reflect the brand through the design.
